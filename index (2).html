About Dr Laith Barnouti

A Surgeon of Global Training & Local Dedication

Dr Laith Barnouti is a highly distinguished Specialist Plastic and Reconstructive Surgeon based in Sydney, Australia. With over 15 years of expert surgical experience, he has performed thousands of successful procedures, earning a reputation for technical excellence and a refined aesthetic eye.

As a Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons (FRACS), Dr Barnouti represents the highest level of surgical accreditation in Australia. His professional foundation is built on extensive international training, having completed advanced fellowships in world-renowned surgical hubs:

Sweden — Advanced Reconstructive Techniques
Brazil — Aesthetic & Body Contouring Surgery
Belgium — Microsurgery & Facial Procedures
USA — Cutting-Edge Surgical Innovation

Beyond his clinical work, Dr Barnouti is a Conjoint Senior Lecturer at UNSW and a frequent media commentator, advocating for the highest ethical standards and patient safety within the Australian cosmetic industry.

Core Competencies

Six Pillars of Surgical Excellence

Dr Laith Barnouti's practice is built upon six interconnected competencies that together deliver results of the highest clinical and aesthetic quality — ensuring every patient receives a comprehensive, safe, and personalised surgical experience.

01

Advanced Facial Rejuvenation

Expert in Deep-Plane Facelifts, Rhinoplasty, and Blepharoplasty. Dr Barnouti's approach focuses on structural integrity and natural harmony — delivering facial rejuvenation that looks entirely refreshed rather than "operated." Every treatment is tailored to the unique anatomy of the individual patient, prioritising long-term, balanced results that honour their natural facial character.

02

Specialist Breast Surgery

With a deep understanding of aesthetic proportions, Dr Barnouti excels in Breast Augmentation, Mastopexy (breast lift), and complex Breast Revision surgery. He utilises world-class techniques to ensure beautiful, symmetrical results while rigorously prioritising patient health and long-term wellbeing, tailoring every procedure to the individual's lifestyle and aesthetic goals.

03

Precision Body Contouring

Specialising in Abdominoplasty and High-Definition Liposuction, Dr Barnouti combines technical precision with an artistic eye to restore and enhance natural body curves. By focusing on refined contours and smooth transitions, he helps patients achieve a more proportionate silhouette while maintaining the highest standards of safety and clinical excellence throughout.

04

Short-Scar Surgical Innovation

A pioneer in minimal-incision techniques, Dr Barnouti's mastery of advanced "short-scar" methods ensures significantly less tissue trauma and reduced visible scarring. This innovative approach allows for a faster recovery process without any compromise to the aesthetic outcome — allowing patients to return to their daily lives more quickly with discreet, high-quality results.

05

Clinical Safety & Ethics

Operating exclusively in fully accredited Sydney hospitals, Dr Barnouti maintains a rigorous "Safety-First" protocol for every procedure. His practice adheres strictly to Australian clinical standards and ethical medical practices, ensuring every patient receives transparent, evidence-based care supported by professional medical teams and comprehensive post-operative hospital support.

06

Academic & Media Leadership

As a Conjoint Senior Lecturer at UNSW, Dr Barnouti is dedicated to surgical research and educating the next generation of specialists. As a frequent media commentator on major Australian networks, he advocates for patient rights and industry transparency, ensuring his practice consistently remains at the global forefront of modern plastic and reconstructive surgery.

Areas of Expertise

World-Class Expertise Across Every Discipline

Dr Laith Barnouti's surgical expertise spans the full spectrum of aesthetic and reconstructive procedures, each performed to an international standard of quality, safety, and artistry.

01

Facial Aesthetic Surgery

Deep-Plane Facelifts, Rhinoplasty, and Blepharoplasty performed with a focus on achieving a refreshed, youthful appearance by addressing deep tissue structures. Results are natural and consistent with the patient's unique facial character — without the typical "pulled" look of traditional surgery.

02

Advanced Breast Procedures

Full spectrum breast surgery including Augmentation, Reductions, Mastopexy, and complex Revision cases. Dr Barnouti is particularly sought after for his advanced techniques in correcting previous outcomes and restoring both aesthetic form and patient comfort with exceptional symmetry and proportion.

03

Modern Body Contouring

High-definition Liposuction and Abdominoplasty (Tummy Tuck) to restore body harmony. Expert in post-weight loss transformations, removing excess skin and tightening underlying muscles. Every procedure delivers smoothly refined contours, providing a more toned and proportionate physical silhouette.

04

Short-Scar Specialist Techniques

A hallmark of Dr Barnouti's expertise is the implementation of short-scar techniques across all procedures. These minimal-incision methods minimise visible scarring and significantly reduce patient recovery time, allowing patients to return to daily life faster while enjoying high-quality, discreet results that prioritise both aesthetics and physical healing.
